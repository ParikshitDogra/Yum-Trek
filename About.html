About Yum Trek

Welcome to Yum Trek, a culinary journey filled with flavors, experiments, and exciting recipes!
My name is Parikshit Dogra, and I’m a 1st-year BTech CSE student with a passion for cooking.
While studying computer science, I’ve also nurtured a deep interest in food, cooking, and discovering new dishes from all over the world.

Why I Started Yum Trek

Yum Trek was born from my love for experimenting in the kitchen and my curiosity to explore different cuisines.
As someone who enjoys trying out new recipes and putting my own spin on traditional dishes, I wanted to create a space where I could share my cooking adventures, tips, and recipes with other food lovers.

Through this blog, I aim to make cooking fun and accessible to everyone.
Whether you’re a beginner trying out your first recipe or an experienced cook looking for inspiration, Yum Trek has something for everyone.
